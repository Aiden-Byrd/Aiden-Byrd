I would rather be playing Red Dead Redemption 2  

My favorite console game is Red Dead Redmption 2 and my favorite mobile game is Angry Birds. Red Dead Redmption 2 is great because it has an amazing story, spectacular graphics, and endless features. It is very complex and yet so easily enjoyable. Meanwhile, Angry Birds is very simple: graphics aren't amazing, simple plot, and basic gameplay. However, it's beauty is in it's simplicity.


Read Dead 2 is almost universally considered the best story game of all time. It's extended developement made an almost bullet-proof game that is timeless. The tale it weaves so expertly is incredible and the replayability of the game is excellent. The game is loved by all and very highly rated as it should be. Angry Birds also is well loved, especially by those that partook in the mobile craze of 2010-2013 (including the reviewers and I). It's simplicity makes it easy for anyone to pick up and enjoy. It is truly a legend of mobile gaming.
